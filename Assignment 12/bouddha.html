Boudhanath Stupa

Boudhanath is one of the largest stupas in Nepal, symbolizing Tibetan Buddhism. It is a center for meditation and prayer. Situated in Kathmandu, Boudhanath is easily accessible. Walk around the stupa, spin prayer wheels, and visit monasteries. It's a serene place for reflection and spiritual exploration.

Boudhanath Stupa, one of the largest stupas in Nepal, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a focal point for Tibetan Buddhism. Tourists at Boudhanath can circumambulate the stupa, spin prayer wheels, and absorb the spiritual ambiance. The area is dotted with monasteries, offering opportunities for cultural exploration. Nearby, the Pashupatinath Temple, with its sacred ghats and Hindu rituals, provides a contrasting yet complementary experience.
